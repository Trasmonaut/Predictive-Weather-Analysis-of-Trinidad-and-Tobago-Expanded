1. Prediction Accuracy:

Our predictor may produce inaccurate or incomplete data. Please consult authoritative and official sources for critical or time-sensitive dates. 2. Data Collection and Usage:

We do not actively measure or collect data in real-time. Our predictions rely solely upon historical data, and as such, they may not reflect current or future conditions accurately. Data used in predictions may also include synthetic or extrapolated datasets to fill in missing information.

3. Time Series Limitations:

Time series predictions become increasingly uncertain as the prediction timeframe extends further into the future. Our data is only accurate up to 14th May 2025, and any predictions beyond this date should be treated with caution. The reliability of predictions may decrease rapidly for dates further in the future, especially during volatile weather seasons.

All data utilized in our predictions is sourced from publicly available historical records. We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of data provided by third-party sources. The predictions provided by TT Weather Predict are powered by the XGBoost algorithm, a popular and robust machine learning model known for its accuracy and efficiency in handling large datasets.
